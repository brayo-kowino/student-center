About Us

Our Mission: To empower students by providing innovative digital tools and support, helping them manage their academic and extracurricular life efficiently.

Vision: To become the leading digital solution for students, offering a platform that simplifies their academic tasks and enriches their learning experiences.

Company History

Founded in 2023 by Brian Odhiambo, a tech enthusiast and Mining Engineering student, Student Center was born out of the desire to solve the unique challenges students face in managing their academic tasks. What started as a simple idea to create assignment management tools quickly expanded into a fully-fledged digital platform. Today, Student Center offers a range of services including a fully operational communication and socialization platform, The SC Space, assignment management, research tools, and a custom Android application designed to make students' lives easier. Our vision is to continue developing innovative solutions that empower students around the world.

Our Services

  • Communication & Socialization - We provide a platform where students can connect, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects.
  • Research & Analysis Tools - Tools designed to help students conduct in-depth research and analysis for academic and personal projects.
  • Android Application & Web Development - We offer custom Android and web development services, tailored for students and academic institutions.
  • Assignment Management - A comprehensive tool to help students manage their assignments, set reminders, and track deadlines.
  • Media & Cyber Services - We provide media production services as well as basic cyber services like website creation and computer training.
  • Custom Student Reports - Tailored reports that analyze student performance, helping them to track their academic progress and plan for improvement.
