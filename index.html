About Iskify360

Knowledge is the fuel. We build the engine.

Iskify360 was founded by Brian Odhiambo in June 2024, based in Westlands, Nairobi. We started as an engineering and software practice with a simple idea: most companies need more than one specialist to get a project done right, and switching between vendors wastes time and money.

So we built one team that covers software, engineering design, cyber security, and documentation. Whether you need an app built, a mechanical part modeled in 3D, a network audited, or a report written to a professional standard, you work with people who already understand your project from the first conversation.

Our story so far

  1. June 2024

    Iskify360 is founded

    Brian Odhiambo starts Iskify360 in Nairobi as an engineering and software practice, with the first version of the Connexa platform in development.

  2. Late 2024

    First products launch

    Assignmate and Echo360 go live, alongside the Iskify360 Android app. Cyber security and documentation services are added to the offering.

  3. Mid 2025

    Sonar v1.0 released

    The first public version of Sonar is released, with a focus on secure messaging and collaboration for small teams.

  4. 2025 – today

    Serving clients across disciplines

    We take on engineering design, software, and security projects for outside clients, while continuing to grow our own product line.

  5. In progress

    New internal tools

    Our engineers are building the next set of internal applications. Details will be shared as they're ready to launch.
