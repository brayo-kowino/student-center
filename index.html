About US

Our Mission

To empower students by offering innovative digital solutions that streamline their academic responsibilities and support their extracurricular activities, ultimately enhancing their educational journey.

Our Vision

To become the leading global platform for students, recognized for providing essential tools that simplify academic life and contribute to personal and professional growth.

Our Story

Established in June 2024 by Brian Odhiambo, a dedicated tech enthusiast and student of Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering, CampusX Digital began with a singular focus: to address the academic management needs of students. What started as a basic assignment management tool evolved into a comprehensive digital platform offering services ranging from communication and socialization, research tools to custom Android applications. Our commitment to innovation continues to drive us forward, as we develop tools that support students' success across the world.

Our Journey

June 2024 - XCenter Launch

We launched the XCenter app, initially offering basic features to support student needs.

August 2024 - Added Assignment Management & Report Generation

Expanded our offerings with tools for managing assignments and generating custom reports.

September 2024 - Xc Online Launch

Launched Xc Online, creating a dedicated platform for student socialization and communication.

October 2024 - Website Launch

Established an online presence by launching our official website, broadening our reach to students globally.

Our Services

Communication & Socialization

Our platform, Xc Online fosters student collaboration, allowing them to share knowledge, connect with peers, and work on joint projects seamlessly.

Research & Analysis Tools

Equip yourself with cutting-edge tools for in-depth research and analysis, whether for academic assignments or personal projects.

Android Application & Web Development

We specialize in developing tailored Android and web applications designed to meet the unique needs of students and educational institutions.

Assignment Management

Organize your assignments effortlessly with our comprehensive toolset, designed to help you track deadlines, set reminders, and stay on top of your work.

Custom Student Reports
