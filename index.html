Who We Are

Empowering the Future Through Technology

Founded in June 2024 by Brian Odhiambo, Iskify360 started as a solution for student assignments and has evolved into a complete digital ecosystem.

The name blends "Iski", a tribute to Brian’s mother, with "360", representing our full-circle approach to solving challenges.

Our Mission

To create an all-in-one platform that fuels curiosity, enhances productivity, and fosters continuous learning.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform driving digital transformation where innovation meets social connection.
