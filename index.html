About ISKIFY360

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations through innovative, accessible, and integrated digital solutions that foster continuous learning, connectivity, and personal growth. At Iskify360, we aim to create an all-in-one platform that fuels curiosity, enhances productivity, and brings the world of knowledge, entertainment, and communication closer together.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform that drives digital transformation, where innovation meets learning, social connection, and entertainment. We envision a world where people can seamlessly access the tools, resources, and communities they need to thrive, all within a single, dynamic platform.

Our Story

Iskify360 was founded in June 2024 by Brian Odhiambo, a student of Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering and a passionate tech enthusiast. Driven by an insatiable curiosity and a desire to solve real-world challenges, Brian envisioned a platform that could empower students and learners everywhere. What began as a simple solution to help students manage assignments evolved into a comprehensive digital ecosystem.

At its core, Iskify360 is a reflection of Brian’s belief in the power of technology to inspire learning, innovation, and collaboration. The platform combines educational tools, media consumption, research resources, and social networking features to foster growth, connectivity, and knowledge sharing. As a student himself, Brian understands the need for accessible, versatile tools that promote success, and this vision continues to shape the platform's development, ensuring it meets the diverse needs of students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts across the globe.

Now, about the name—why "Iskify360"? Well, "Iski" comes from the founder’s mom, Iska, because what better way to honor a strong maternal influence than by sneaking her name into a tech platform, right? As for "360"—that’s just to keep things interesting. It represents the full-circle approach we take to innovation—thinking of every angle, covering all bases, and ensuring we're always evolving. So, in a nutshell, Iskify360 means we’re all about offering a complete solution while keeping things grounded in family, fun, and a bit of tech magic. And if you’ve got a curious mind and a love for technology, you’re exactly who we built this for!

Our Journey

June 2024 - iskify360 Launch

We launched the i360 android application, initially offering basic features to support basic media access such as music and video player.

August 2024 - Added Assignment Management & Report Generation

Expanded our offerings with tools for managing assignments and generating custom reports.

September 2024 - i360 Online Launch

Launched i360 Online, creating a dedicated platform for socialization and communication.

October 2024 - Website Launch

Established an online presence by launching our official website, broadening our reach to users globally.
