history_edu Our Story

Mixing Engineering with Modern Tech.

Started in June 2024, Iskify360 was built to combine hard engineering skills with modern computer software.

Our founder, Brian Odhiambo, saw a big need while studying Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). The engineering field needs exact measurements and tough calculations. But it also needs good software to organize and show that data clearly.

Iskify360 fixes this problem. We take the careful planning used in heavy engineering and apply it to building apps, websites, and keeping data safe.
