Installation Instructions

  1. Download the APK file by clicking the button above.
  2. Open your device's settings and navigate to Security or Privacy.
  3. Enable "Install from unknown sources" to allow installation of apps outside Google Play Store.
  4. Locate the downloaded APK file in your file manager and tap to install.
  5. Follow on-screen prompts to complete installation.
  6. If the installation is blocked by PLAY PROTECT, go to play store settings, disable play protect scaning, then proceed or staart over again.
