Echo360 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Echo360 ("the App") handles information when you use our media player. We value your privacy and are committed to transparency and minimal data collection.

2. Information We Collect

Echo360 is designed to function primarily as an offline media player. We collect only limited information necessary to improve performance.

  • Local Media Access: The App scans media files stored on your device in order to display and play them. These files never leave your device.
  • Microphone Access: Used exclusively for real-time audio visualizers. Echo360 does not record, store, or transmit microphone audio.
  • Diagnostics Data: Anonymous crash reports and performance logs may be collected to improve stability and playback quality.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not record microphone audio.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers such as name, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not upload or store your media files.
  • We do not track your browsing activity.

4. How We Use Information

Any collected data is used solely to:

  • Ensure smooth audio and video playback
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and crashes

5. Data Sharing

Echo360 does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Diagnostic data, if collected, is anonymized and used only for technical improvement.

6. User Control

You can grant or revoke permissions at any time through your device settings. Certain features (such as audio visualizers) may not function without required permissions.

7. Children’s Privacy

Echo360 is not designed to knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
