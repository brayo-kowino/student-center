Getting Started

To begin using Connexa services, please ensure you have completed the secure authentication process.

  • 1. Agree to Policy Review and accept our terms to ensure a safe and compliant experience.
  • 2. Create Account Register using your professional email to set up your primary profile.
  • 3. Verification Access your account securely after verifying your identity credentials.

Account Management

You have full control over managing your account, updating settings, and enhancing security. Connexa maintains strict data integrity protocols regarding your account information.

  • Profile Updates Modify your personal details, bio, and visual identity settings.
  • Security Reset Initiate a secure password reset if you suspect unauthorized access.
  • Deactivation Control your account status. Note that Connexa reserves the right to suspend accounts found in violation of our Terms.
